Rembrandt At The Rijksmuseum

Rembrandt.
Dutch, Jul 15, 1606 - Oct 4, 1669. Born Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn, Harmenszoon indicates that his father was named Harmen, and van Rijn refers to his hometown near the Rhine River. Considered one of the greatest artists in the history of art. The greatest of the Dutch Golden Age. Extremely prolific and innovative, he gave rise to important new genres. Also known as a painter of ligh and shade.
